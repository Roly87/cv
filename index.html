Roly Del Rio

Former Assistant Cruise Director for Carnival Cruise Lines

My name is Rolando Del Rio and I currently reside in Wilton Manors, Florida. For many years of my life I enjoyed working for Carnival Cruise Lines aboard nine different Carnival ships and visited over 30 different port of calls in over a dozen different countries. I began my role as a Cruise Staff employee, hosting trivia's and assisting with day to day activities, and then quickly moved up to the role of Assistant Cruise Director which was one of the most wonderful experiences of my life. I was in charge of overseeing the activities scheduling for all onboard guest entertainment, as well as host talk shows, bingo, make public announcements, run safety drills, and much more.
